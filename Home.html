New Courses

AI

AI, or artificial intelligence, refers to machines and software that mimic human cognitive functions such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. It encompasses technologies like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, enabling applications in various fields, from healthcare to finance, transforming how we interact with the digital world.

ML

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn from data and improve over time without explicit programming. By using algorithms to identify patterns and make predictions, ML is applied in various domains, including healthcare, finance, and marketing, revolutionizing decision-making and automating processes.

CSE

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is an interdisciplinary field that combines computer science principles with engineering practices. It focuses on the design, development, and analysis of computer systems and software. CSE encompasses programming, algorithms, data structures, networking, and artificial intelligence, preparing students for diverse careers in technology and innovation.
